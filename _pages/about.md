Hi, I'm Yunbei Zhang, a second-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at Tulane University, advised by Prof. [Jihun Hamm](https://www.cs.tulane.edu/~jhamm3/). I received B.S. in Mathematics from Osaka University in Japan, advised by Prof. [Masaaki Wada](http://www4.math.sci.osaka-u.ac.jp/~wada/index.html).

My research interests are broadly in machine learning and mainly focus on: **Robust Machine Learning** and **Out-of-Distribution Generalization**.
